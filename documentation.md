Differences to note:

In the original crypto.py made by Mansi, our master key was generated by using the os module.
This master key was then encoded and saved in a file.

In the updated version, we are using the secrets module instead of the os module, and applying b64 encoding on it to convert it into a human readable version and save it as our master key. Although, there's no major difference between the two methods, it is generally recommended to use this.
